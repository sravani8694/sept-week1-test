Explore City of GODS

Mathura is the birthplace of Krishna,which is located at the Krishna Janmasthan Temple Complex.It is one of the Sapta Puri, the seven cities considered holy by Hindus, also is called Mokshyadayni Tirth.Mathura has been chosen as one of the heritage cities for the Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana scheme of Government of India.
